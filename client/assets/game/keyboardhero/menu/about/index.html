About Keyboard Hero

Keyboard Hero is an entertaining typing game developed by the Alpha Team for our Software Engineering final project. Our goal is to create an engaging platform where players can enjoy a Guitar Hero-style experience using pre-uploaded music or their own tracks.

Instructions on How to Play

Welcome to Keyboard Hero! Follow these simple steps to dive into the action:

  • Get Started: Begin by clicking "Level Select" to choose a challenge or upload your own song for a personalized experience. Once you're ready, press "Start" to launch the game!
  • Set Your Keys: Use the default keys, DFJK, to play, or customize your own key setup in the settings menu to suit your style.
  • Score Points: As the keys cascade down the screen, press the corresponding key in the correct column to earn points. Hit multiple keys in succession to build up your score multiplier!
  • Track Your Progress: Keep an eye on your score and aim to save it to the scoreboard to showcase your skills to the world!

Project Overview

In the fast-paced world of digital entertainment, Keyboard Hero offers a fun way to enjoy music and gaming simultaneously. Players can experience the thrill of a guitar hero-style game while enjoying their favorite tunes or personal tracks.
